Partner with ExamFund — Sponsor exam support & student success

ExamFund helps students prepare and covers small, urgent exam‑time needs. Our platform includes an AI Study Partner, timed mock exams & simulations, a notes & marketplace, tutor marketplace, and a Study Wallet that receives sponsored micro‑grants.

Why sponsor

  • Direct social impact: fund micro‑grants to cover exam fees, data, transport, and materials.
  • Recruitment & brand reach: engage high‑performing students with your employer/scholarship pipeline.
  • Measurable CSR: receive aggregated KPI reporting and student testimonials.

How funds are used

  • Micro‑scholarships / exam grants credited to Study Wallets
  • Sponsored data bundles for remote learners
  • Paid tutoring credits & verified learning incentives

Pilot timeline

  1. Week 0 – Sponsor onboarding
  2. Week 1 – Payment & wallet integration
  3. Week 2 – Soft launch (pilot cohort)
  4. Weeks 3–6 – Active campaign
  5. Week 8 – Impact report
