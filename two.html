How ExamGuard Rewards Students
ExamGuard Nigeria motivates active academic engagement by awarding credit points to students for:
  • Completing mock tests and official exam simulations
  • Practicing past and practice questions
  • Writing or reviewing educational articles and blog posts
Credit points are instantly credited to each student's Study Wallet.
  • Students can convert credits to withdrawable cash (direct to bank or airtime)
  • Or use points to make purchases in the ExamGuard online marketplace (textbooks, gadgets, essentials) — often at subsidized, student-friendly rates.
This creates a genuine link between academic diligence and real financial reward/support.
